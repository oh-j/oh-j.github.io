  • About Me

    Hello, I'm on the road to becoming an Urban Planner. What kind of planner you ask? I'm not entirely sure. I can only tell you with confidence that my interests lie in producing aesthetically pleasing and/or socially beneficial products. In this context the word "products" can be read as concepts, ideas, services, tangible goods, or any combination of the aforementioned.

    I love to participate in problem solving/design compeitions to create innovate solutions for complex issues, and thrive off of sharing and absorbing new information with others. I'm currently participating in the Biomimicry Global Design Challenge hosted by the Biomimicry Institute with my excellent friend Vivian Ho.

    If youre participating in a competition that you think I may be able to help you with, please do not hesitate to send me a message! I'm always on the lookout for a good challenge.

    I would also enjoy the opportunity to view your website and perhaps talk to good people about cool things. If you have a website to show or wish to have a chat, once again, send over a message!
